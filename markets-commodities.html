Commodity Macro Drivers

🥇 Gold — Inversely correlated with US dollar and real yields. Safe-haven flows during geopolitical stress. Central bank buying is a structural tailwind.

🥈 Silver — Industrial demand (solar panels, electronics) plus monetary metal characteristics. Higher volatility than gold.

🛢 Crude Oil — OPEC+ production policy, US shale supply, and global demand drive price action. Watch Cushing inventories and EIA reports.

🔥 Natural Gas — Highly seasonal. Storage levels, weather forecasts, and LNG export demand are key.
