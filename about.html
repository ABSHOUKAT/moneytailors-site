Our Story

About MoneyTailors

An independent finance media brand built for traders and investors in the GCC, Pakistan, and beyond.

Built by a trader, for traders.

MoneyTailors was created out of a simple frustration: the best financial tools, analysis platforms, and market data are either locked behind expensive subscriptions or buried in platforms not built with GCC and South Asian markets in mind.

We built MoneyTailors to bridge that gap. Every tool on this platform is free. Every market we cover — PSX, Tadawul, Forex, US equities — is chosen because it matters to our audience.

Whether you are a salaried professional in Riyadh building a portfolio on the side, a trader in Karachi tracking KSE-100 momentum, or a retail forex trader managing risk — MoneyTailors is built around your financial reality.
