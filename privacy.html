Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

MoneyTailors collects minimal information. When you submit the contact form, we collect your name and email address for the sole purpose of responding to your message. We do not collect this data automatically or build user profiles.

This website uses TradingView widgets to display live market data. TradingView may set cookies or collect usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We have no control over TradingView's data practices. We also use Web3Forms to process contact form submissions — form data is transmitted to Web3Forms' servers to deliver it to our inbox.

This website embeds YouTube videos. When you interact with embedded videos, YouTube (Google) may collect data per their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy at google.com/privacy.

4. Cookies

MoneyTailors does not set first-party cookies. Third-party widgets (TradingView, YouTube) may set cookies through their embedded content. You can manage these via your browser settings.

We do not operate a database of user information. Contact form submissions are delivered to our inbox via Web3Forms and not stored on any MoneyTailors server.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact
